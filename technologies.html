Application of Data Science in Technology

Data scientists work with complex technologies such as:

Artificial Intelligence

Machine learning models and related software are used to perform predictive and prescriptive analyses

Cloud Computing

Cloud technologies provide data scientists with the flexibility and processing power they need to perform advanced data analytics.

Internet of Things

IoT describes a variety of gadgets that have the ability to automatically connect to the internet. These gadgets accumulate information for data science projects. They produce vast amounts of data that can be utilized for data extraction and mining.

Quantum Computing

Complex computations are executed at high speeds by quantum computers. They are used by skilled data scientists to create intricate quantitative algorithms.
