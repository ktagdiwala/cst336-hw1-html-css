What is Data Science?

Data science is the study of data to extract valuable insights from vast data sets. Data science requires an understanding of the practical application of several fields- including mathematics, statistics, artificial intelligence, and computer engineering- to analyze large amounts of data. The results of these analyses allow data scientists to recognize patterns in collected data and use those findings to make predictions about future data.
